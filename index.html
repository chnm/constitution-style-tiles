Historic

Color scheme and typography inspired by the ship itself. We highlight the red, green, and black paints of the ship, and will use accents evocative of its rigging, lines, and scrollwork. We have chosen a sans serif font, Monda, which draws from the lettering on the ship's stern. A readable serifed font, PT Serif, is used for the body copy, imbuing a historical feel to the text.
